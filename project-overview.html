Lambda Launcher

Mar 2023 to - (Ongoing) | C#, WPF

An independently developed WPF/C# project, a handy launcher for the Windows platform. Users wakeup Lambda Launcher with a hotkey, then press only one another key to instantly launch frequently used applications, significantly reducing the number of keystrokes required to open an app.
